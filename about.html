Organisation History

Founded in 1888 by John Boyd Dunlop in Scotland/Ireland, Dunlop revolutionized transportation by inventing the first practical pneumatic (air-filled) tire to make tricycles more comfortable. The company quickly became a global powerhouse in pneumatic tyre production, pioneering innovations like run-flat technology and dominating racing circuits like Le Mans.
