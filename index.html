Introduction
 
      
 
       Dunlop's target audience is a broad spectrum of motorists looking for
       reliable, high-performance tyres at accessible price points, covering
       everyday drivers, SUV owners, and commercial fleet managers. 
       The brand targets safety-conscious consumers.
    
 
    
 
       Drive Sales and Consumer Engagement:Provide instant pricing on tyres,
       promote the "Click2Fit" partner program for online purchases, and offer
       a"Request a Quote" feature that directs users to local, authorized dealers. 




        New Brand Direction:As of Dec 3, 2025, Dunlop announced a new global brand
         statement,“TAKING YOU BEYOND,”aimed at unifying communication, enhancing brand value,
          and accelerating international growth. 




       Regional Focus (SA):The South African website highlights
      a"rich heritage" (130+ years) and positions itself as a
       market leader, having been voted the #1 Tyre Brand by consumers multiple times. 




      Core Pillars:The website focuses on accessibility, safety, and empowering local entrepreneurs,
      particularly through its township and rural dealer network.
